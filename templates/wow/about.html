About Space Goats CoastToCoast

Space Goats CoastToCoast is an Alliance World of Warcraft guild on US Whisperwind started by a small group of friends. Thanks for checking out our site! Guild History:

Many of our members returned to the game in late Cataclysm. We joined Auchindoun to play with a friend there, and joined the guild he was in, Open Ticket. A large guild of over 500 characters, Open Ticket was very welcoming, but its size made it difficult to get spots in raids and to get to know people. After playing for a while we decided we really wanted to be our own group. We bought an existing guild whose members had left for SWTOR and transfered it over to the Alliance. And so, Space Goats CoastToCoast came to be.

We do not own or claim to own any show with a similar name to our guild


Current Guild Activity:

With the launch of MOP we are currently getting our members leveled and geared for the new content! Our current raid time is Monday at 7:30PM CST and we have a ten man group getting ready. Many of our members are also expressing interest in the new Challenge Mode Dungeons. We are currently running these on an ad hoc basis. Once more guildees are leveled we may consider having dedicated groups or dedicated nights. You can check what events are currently scheduled on our homepage!
